.chk
.chk label
Calculates an 8-bit checksum made up of the sum of all the data between the current instruction pointer and the specified label.
----
Warning: does not produce the same results as TASM.
----
====
_label:
    .db 1
    .db 4
    .db 54
    .chk _label ; Has a value of 59 with Brass, 51 in TASM.
====
