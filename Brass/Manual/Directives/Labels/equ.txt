.equ/=
label .equ value
This assigns a label with a particular value, so you can then use the label in expressions rather than the constant each time.
====
size_of_array .equ 30
and_factor = %00001111

    ; ...

    ld b, size_of_array
_loop:
    ld a, (hl)
    call do_something
    and and_factor
    ld (hl), a
    inc hl
    djnz _loop
====
